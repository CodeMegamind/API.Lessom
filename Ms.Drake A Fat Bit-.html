Tim Berners-Lee and His First Web Page

The world's first web page was created by English scientist Tim Berners-Lee on August 6, 1991, and it still exists. The page contains information about what the World Wide Web is, how to install a web server, how to download a browser, and other background information.

ow many people use the Internet?

At the beginning of 2020, more than 4.5 billion people used the Internet. At the same time, the total population of the Earth is estimated at 7.8 billion people, that is, a significant part of humanity has not yet connected to the Internet.
