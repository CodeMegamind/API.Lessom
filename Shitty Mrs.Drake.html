IEEE Published a New Programming Languages Ranking

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE Spectrum) recently released its ranking of programming languages for 2020, in which Python traditionally takes the lead.

Top three finalists:

List of the Most Popular IT Professions

The research was conducted based on the descriptions of more than 300,000 IT job vacancies in major cities published between 2016 and 2018.

List of professions:

  • Programmer
  • Tester
  • System Administrator

  • System Analyst
